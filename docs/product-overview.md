# Product Overview

## Positioning
LUXECAST is a premium digital talent operating system for models, actors, ambassadors, creators, and event talent.

## User Roles
### Talent
- creates profile
- uploads portfolio
- manages availability
- pays subscription
- accepts bookings
- receives payouts

### Client
- searches talent
- posts casting calls
- books and pays
- reviews talent
- rebooks through platform

### Admin
- approves profiles
- manages revenue and disputes
- enforces anti-bypass rules
- monitors quality and risk

## Revenue
- recurring talent subscription
- commission on completed bookings
- upsells: featured listings, premium verification, urgent casting

## Core Promise
Premium talent, controlled bookings, protected payments, strong reputation, and platform enforcement.
