# Monetization

## Base Pricing
- Talent subscription: $59 / week
- Platform commission: 12% baseline
- Featured placement: premium add-on
- Urgent casting boost: premium add-on

## Sample Projection
At 500 active talent:
- listing revenue: 500 x $59 = $29,500 weekly
- average booking volume per active talent: $1,350 weekly
- 12% commission: $162 per talent weekly
- commission revenue: ~$81,000 weekly
- total weekly platform revenue: ~$110,500
